About

We are a non-profit organization for all artists to make money off their craft. We have a collective group of organizers that host free events and art galleries for local artist to come and sell their artwork! We also host craft-fairs and are afflicated with companies such as Hobby Lobby to seek out artists to be afflicates. We...

  1. Help artists make passive income
  2. Let artists be seen
  3. Connect you with artists in your area

Income, Exposure and Community are all important things any artists needs to turn a passion into a career! Many artists struggled to make a living off their work but "Artist Support Center" wants to change that!

But I am a Artist?

Oh so you are? Then send us a email! Send us your art page (social media platform you are on), you information, and location and we will connect you to one of our many art comunities around the states!

Do I have to draw?

Of course not! Art can be writing, painting and music! Anything you are creative in can give you exposure and passive income!
